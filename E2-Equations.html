Equations for Exam Two

Position velocity acceleration

\[\vec{v} = \lim_{\Delta t\to 0} \frac{\Delta \vec{x}}{\Delta t} = \frac{d \vec{x}}{dt}\] \[\vec{a} = \lim_{\Delta t\to 0} \frac{\Delta \vec{v}}{\Delta t} = \frac{d \vec{v}}{dt}\]

Kinematics: Constant acceleration

\[\vec{x}_f = \vec{x}_i + \vec{v}_i \, t + \frac12 \vec{a}\,t^2\] \[\vec{v}_f = \vec{v}_i + \vec{a}\,t\] \[\vec{v}_f^2 = \vec{v}_i^2 + 2\,\vec{a} (\vec{x}_f - \vec{x}_i)\]

Circular Motion

\[ a_c= \frac{v^2}{r}\] \[T = \frac{2 \pi r}{v}\] \[ \omega = \frac{2 \pi}{T} \] \[ x = r \, \theta \qquad v = r \, \omega \qquad a = r \, \alpha\] \[a_c = \frac{v^2}{r}\] \[ a = \sqrt{a_t^2 + a_c^2}\]

Forces

\[\sum \vec{F} = m \,\vec{a}\] \[f=\mu N\]

Work and Energy

\[W = F \Delta r \cos(\theta)\] \[K=\frac12 mv^2\] \[U_g = mgy\] \[U_s= \frac12 kx^2\] \[U_R= \frac12 I \omega^2\] \[W_{nc} = \Delta K + \Delta U\] \[U_f - U_i = -\int F \, dx\] \[\mathcal{P} = \frac{dE}{dt}\]

Momentum

\[\vec{p} = m \vec{v}\] \[\vec{I} = \int_{t_i}^{t_f} \sum \vec{F} dt\] \[\vec{r}_{CM} = \frac1M \sum_i m_i \vec{r}_i\] \[\Delta \vec{p}_{tot} = 0\] \[ \vec{F} = \frac{d\vec{p}}{dt}\] \[m_1 \, \vec{v}_{1i} +m_2 \, \vec{v}_{2i} = m_1 \, \vec{v}_{1f} +m_2 \, \vec{v}_{2f}\] \[v_{1i} + v_{1f} = v_{2i} + v_{2f}\]
