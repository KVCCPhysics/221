Homework C - Oscillations

Problem C.1

In an engine, a piston oscillates with simple harmonic motion so that its position varies according to the expression \[ x = 5.00~\cos \left( 2t + \frac{\pi}{6} \right) \] where x is in centimeters and t is in seconds. At \(t = 0\), find

  1. the position of the particle
  2. its velocity
  3. its acceleration
  4. find the period of oscillations
  5. find the amplitude of the motion

Problem C.2

  2. the speed of the object when its position is \(1.00~cm\).
  3. the kinetic energy when its position is \(3.00~cm\)
  4. the potential energy when its position is \(3.00~cm\).
