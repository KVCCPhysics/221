Lab 6 - Torque

In this lab, you will investigate torque using a massive beam suspended in static equilibrium. This experiment will give some practice in how to set up the system of equations and solve for an unknown force. In the second section of the lab, you will perform some algebraic manipulation to ’linearize" the system (I dislike this term, but you may see it in the future). Finally you will create a figure displaying the theoretical and measured results.
